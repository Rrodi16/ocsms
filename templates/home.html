Our Mission

The Online Cost Sharing Management System (OCSMS) is designed to streamline the cost sharing processes at Mekdela Amba University. Our mission is to provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for students and staff to manage cost sharing agreements and payments.

Our system integrates modern technology with proven financial management practices to create a seamless experience for all users.

Frequently Asked Questions

To create a cost sharing agreement, log in to your student account, navigate to the "Cost Sharing" section, and fill out the online form with your details.

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash payments at designated university offices. All online payments are processed securely.

You can check your payment status by logging into your account and navigating to the "Payment History" section. All your payments and their status will be displayed there.
